Our Story

From the Heart
of the Aegean

Founded in 2010, Kreta was born out of a deep love for the flavors, aromas, and traditions of the Greek island of Crete.

We use only the finest olive oils, fresh herbs, and locally sourced ingredients to create dishes that transport you straight to the Mediterranean coast.
